About Stock It Inventory

Stock It Inventory is a leading inventory management application designed to streamline stock management processes for businesses of all sizes. Committed to delivering top-notch inventory solutions, our app is tailored to meet the dynamic needs of modern-day businesses, ensuring they operate at peak efficiency.

Our Services and Features

  • Comprehensive Inventory Management: Easily manage and track your stock levels, purchases, sales, and daily profit with user-friendly features and robust functionalities.
  • GST Management: The application supports the management of GST bills and parties, enhancing compliance with taxation norms.
  • Multiple Platforms: Currently available on Android, with desktop and iOS versions under development.
  • Backup and Security: Your data is securely stored in the cloud, ensuring accessibility and protection against data loss.
  • Payment Solutions: We offer versatile payment methodologies including UPI, PhonePe, and Google Pay, ensuring seamless and secure transactions.
  • Sales and Purchase Management: Efficiently handle sales returns, categorize items, and manage spare parts inventory for sectors like the 2-wheeler EV market.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design allows users to easily add products, create bills, edit transactions, and maintain comprehensive records.
  • Customer Support: A dedicated support team is available through direct messaging within the app, equipped to resolve any issues, offer guidance, and receive feedback.
